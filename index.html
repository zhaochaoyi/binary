Reading the calculator: a focused asset wins a criterion when its input p* exceeds that criterion's break-even p-star threshold. The thresholds are not meant to agree with each other: expected NPV is a mean-value criterion, loss probability is a downside-tail criterion, Sharpe is a mean-risk criterion, and log utility is preference based.
